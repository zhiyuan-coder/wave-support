1. Introduction

Welcome to GifSnip. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe, secure experience.

Key Point: GifSnip processes all content locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your videos, GIFs, or personal data.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

GifSnip is designed with a privacy-first approach. We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Videos you select from your photo library
  • GIFs you create using the app
  • Images or text you add to your GIFs
  • Any personal information or identifiers
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Location information
  • Device information

3. No Internet Connection Required

GifSnip operates entirely offline. The app does not require an internet connection to function and does not transmit any data over the network.

4. Local Processing Only

All operations in GifSnip are performed entirely on your device:

  • Video analysis and processing
  • GIF generation and encoding
  • Frame editing and manipulation
  • Text and image overlay rendering
  • All export operations

Your content never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

5. App Permissions

GifSnip requests minimal permissions:

Photo Library Access

  • Purpose: To allow you to select videos for GIF creation and save completed GIFs to your device
  • Usage: We only access the specific videos you select; we do not scan or access your entire photo library
  • Storage: Created GIFs are saved directly to your device's Photos app by the system

8. Data Security

Since all processing occurs locally on your device:
