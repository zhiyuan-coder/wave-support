Privacy Policy

Photo Privacy Tool
Last Updated: February 2025

Summary: Photo Privacy Tool processes all your photos locally on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of your photos or personal data.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Photo Privacy Tool ("we," "our," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our App handles information when you use our services.

By using Photo Privacy Tool, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Photo Privacy Tool is designed with privacy as the core principle. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information
  • We do not collect your photos or images
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect device identifiers
  • We do not collect usage analytics or tracking data
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies

3. How We Process Your Photos

All photo processing occurs entirely on your device:

  • Photos you select are processed locally within the App
  • Metadata removal happens entirely on your device
  • Processed photos are saved directly to your photo library
  • Your photos are never uploaded to any server
  • We have no access to your photos at any point

4. Data Storage

Photo Privacy Tool does not store any of your data on external servers. All operations are performed locally on your device. The App does not create any external backups or cloud storage of your information.

5. Third-Party Services

Photo Privacy Tool does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media platforms. We operate completely independently.

6. Location Services

The App uses Apple's built-in geocoding service (CLGeocoder) to convert GPS coordinates from your photos into readable addresses. This service:

  • Is provided by Apple as part of iOS
  • Requires an internet connection to function
  • Is subject to Apple's privacy policy
  • Does not share your data with us

You can use the App without the geocoding feature by not granting location-related permissions.

7. Photo Library Access

The App requests access to your photo library for the following purposes only:

  • Read Access: To select and process photos you choose
  • Write Access: To save processed photos back to your library

The App only accesses photos that you explicitly select. It does not scan or access other photos in your library.

8. Children's Privacy

Photo Privacy Tool is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, our App is appropriate for children. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

9. Security

Since all processing happens locally on your device and we do not collect or store any data, there is minimal security risk. Your photos never leave your device, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

10. Your Rights

Because we do not collect any personal information, there is no data to:

  • Access or export
  • Delete or modify
  • Transfer to another service

You maintain complete control over your data at all times.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify you through the App if significant changes are made

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our App, please contact us at:

Email: support@photoprivacytool.app

13. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), please note:

  • We do not process any personal data that would require a legal basis under GDPR
  • You have no data subject rights to exercise because we hold no personal data
  • No data transfers occur since no data is collected

14. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have certain rights regarding their personal information. Since we do not collect, sell, or share any personal information:

  • There is no personal information to disclose
  • There is no personal information to delete
  • There is no personal information being sold or shared
